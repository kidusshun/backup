About us

We are travel lander founded in 1993 with only one office and few team members. Now we have grown alot, With offices and team members spread across six continents, we are bound by our shared values. This means from our passionate team members designing and creating each trip to our local specialists on the ground in each destination who bring it to life, each of us are connected by the same core beliefs.
